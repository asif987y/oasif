## 🤖 About OBuyer

OBuyer is an intelligent AI-powered shopping assistant that revolutionizes how you shop online. Instead of manually searching through dozens of websites, OBuyer uses artificial intelligence to find the best products, compare prices, and alert you about deals - all through a simple conversational interface.

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎯 AI Product Finder
- Natural language product search
- Semantic understanding of your needs
- Instant relevant product suggestions

### 💰 Smart Price Comparison
- Real-time price tracking across multiple retailers
- Side-by-side product specifications
- AI-generated verdict on best value

### 🔔 Deal Alerts
- Automatic price drop notifications
- Trending deals curated by AI
- Limited-time offer tracking

### 👤 Personalized Recommendations
- ML-based product suggestions
- Budget optimization
- Browsing history analysis

### 📝 AI Product Summaries
- TL;DR of product reviews
- Sentiment analysis from thousands of reviews
- Pros and cons extraction

### 🛡️ Scam Detection
- Trust score for each product
- Seller reliability check
- Fake review detection
